FAQ

here are the most 'Frequently Asked Questions' we get from our community

Customer

Artist

How do I buy music?

You need a MusicBox account and a payment method such as a debit or credit card or PayPal account

How do I publish my music?

You will need to apply for the MusicBox Artist program, from then, you can upload single or full song albums

For how long do I get to keep the music?

It is forever, as long as the service is still running, have access to your MusicBox account or have your own unencrypted digital copy saved

What happens when I remove my albums and/or songs from MusicBox?

Users will no longer be able to buy the original album or song you published, users who have bought your music will still be able to dowloand their own copy from our servers for one year. We will announce this to customers who bought music from you in an email. You can only do or undo this action by calling us through our phone number or email.

What happens when I want to suspend my account?

You will recieve a copy of all your Albums ad songs you have bought from the platform and are free to make backups of it. Please keep in mind we do not refund or cannot undo this interaction 30 days after your suspension request

What happens when I want to suspend my artist account?

Users will no longer be able to buy the original album or song you published, users who have bought your music will still be able to dowloand their own copy from our servers for one year, after this, your profile and albums will be removed from the platform completely. We will announce this to customers who bought music from you in an email.
